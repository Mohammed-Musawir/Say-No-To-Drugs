UNDERSTANDING DIFFERENT DRUGS & THEIR EFFECTS

Learn about various substances and how they impact your body and mind.

Opioids

Including heroin, fentanyl, and prescription painkillers

Effects on the Body:

  • Respiratory depression
  • Extreme physical dependence
  • Risk of fatal overdose
  • Weakened immune system
  • Gastrointestinal issues

Effects on the Mind:

  • Euphoria followed by severe depression
  • Impaired judgment
  • Psychological dependence
  • Anxiety and paranoia

Stimulants

Including cocaine, methamphetamine, and MDMA

Effects on the Body:

  • Increased heart rate and blood pressure
  • Hyperthermia (dangerously high body temperature)
  • Cardiovascular complications
  • Severe weight loss and malnutrition
  • Dental problems ("meth mouth")

Effects on the Mind:

  • Intense euphoria followed by crash
  • Psychosis and hallucinations
  • Aggressive or violent behavior
  • Severe anxiety and paranoia

Hallucinogens

Including LSD, psilocybin, and PCP

Effects on the Body:

  • Increased heart rate and blood pressure
  • Nausea and vomiting
  • Loss of appetite
  • Increased body temperature
  • Impaired coordination

Effects on the Mind:

  • Hallucinations and sensory distortions
  • Altered perception of time
  • Risk of "bad trips" and psychosis
  • Flashbacks (HPPD)

Depressants

Including alcohol, benzodiazepines, and barbiturates

Effects on the Body:

  • Respiratory depression
  • Slowed heart rate
  • Impaired coordination
  • Risk of fatal withdrawal
  • Liver damage (alcohol)

Effects on the Mind:

  • Reduced anxiety initially, increased long-term
  • Impaired memory and cognition
  • Depression
  • Severe psychological dependence

Cannabis

Including marijuana and synthetic cannabinoids

Effects on the Body:

  • Increased heart rate
  • Respiratory issues (when smoked)
  • Impaired coordination
  • Potential lung damage
  • Cannabinoid hyperemesis syndrome

Effects on the Mind:

  • Impaired memory and concentration
  • Anxiety and paranoia (high doses)
  • Potential trigger for psychosis in vulnerable individuals
  • Amotivational syndrome with long-term use

Synthetic Drugs

Including bath salts, spice, and NBOMes

Effects on the Body:

  • Extreme hyperthermia
  • Cardiovascular emergencies
  • Kidney failure
  • Seizures
  • High risk of death

Effects on the Mind:

  • Extreme agitation and aggression
  • Severe psychosis
  • Suicidal thoughts
  • Unpredictable and dangerous behavior

Get Help

SUPPORT IS AVAILABLE

If you or someone you know is struggling with substance abuse, help is available.

National Helpline

1-800-662-HELP (4357)

Free, confidential, 24/7/365 treatment referral and information service (in English and Spanish) for individuals and families facing mental and/or substance use disorders.

Crisis Text Line

Text HOME to 741741

Free 24/7 support for those in crisis. Text from anywhere in the USA to text with a trained Crisis Counselor.
